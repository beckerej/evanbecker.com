Frequently Asked Questions

I aim high at being focused on building relationships with my clients and community.

How does it work?
Once you purchase one of the websites above, you will be emailed a questionnaire to learn more about what you are looking for in a website. From there, we can communicate with each other to design the site to your liking. Delivery is guaranteed within 7 days after the questionnaire is filled out and all information received!
I purchased an E-commerce website. How do I add products to my website?
Once you purchase a E-commerce website and send all basic information to me, within 1 week you will be given a login into the backend of your website so that you can add your products. I can also offer to add them myself at $5/product. I can do this with different payment processors and engines. I will use whatever works best for the project. Your customer's information will always be secured.
What if I don't like the website?
Once you have the first draft of the website, you will be able to make one round of website changes free of charge. If after that edit you are still unhappy you can request additional edits at my standard freelancing rate. If you'd like to add more pages, you can do that at any time using this form.
Why is there a 2 page minimum?
Most websites need at least a Home Page and a way to interact with their audience in the form of a contact page. These are the bare minimum required for any website. If requested, I can put it on one-page styled website, but it's still counted as two pages.
Can I add/upgrade my plan at any time?
Absolutely!
You can request an additional page at anytime using this form.
Can I pay per hour or per contract rather than per page?
Yes!
My standard freelancing rate is $25/hr and you can contact me using this form for contracting a large job.
